VoIP Quick Guide

SIP trunks = your phone lines

  • Carry inbound & outbound calls over the internet.
  • No per-channel fees — we size the channels for you.
  • Works with desk phones, cordless, and the mobile app.

Concurrent calls = calls at the same time

  • Each outside call uses one “line”.
  • Rule of thumb: 4 extensions → 4 concurrent calls.
  • Internal calls between extensions don’t use a line.

One main number, many phones

  • All phones can share one main number.
  • Route calls with ring groups, hunt lists & time rules.
  • Optional direct lines (DDIs) for specific users/departments.
