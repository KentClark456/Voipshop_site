How to Move Your Team from Landlines to VoIP in a Single Day

Switching from outdated landlines to modern VoIP might sound like a major project. But for most small to mid-sized teams, it’s something that can be done in a single day — with the right setup and support.

No Complex Infrastructure

Unlike old-school phone systems that require servers, switches, and specialist cabling, VoIP works over the internet — often using the same connection you already have. That means no construction, rewiring, or downtime.

Pre-Configured Devices

At VoIP Shop, we ship all desk phones and cordless units pre-configured. All your team needs to do is plug them into power and internet — and they’re ready to make and receive calls.

No manual setup. No on-site technician needed. Just plug-and-play simplicity.

Use What You Already Have

Already have hardware or prefer mobile apps? No problem. Our cloud PBX supports:

  • Desk phones (pre-configured)
  • Cordless handsets
  • Mobile softphone apps (iOS & Android)

That means you can mix and match based on each team member’s workflow.

Porting Numbers (Or Start Fresh)

You can keep your existing number through number porting — or get a new local or national number instantly. We’ll guide you through the process without interrupting your business operations.

Support That Doesn’t Slow You Down

Our WhatsApp-based support is fast, personal, and available when you need it. No call centres, no tickets — just real help from real people.

Need us to walk you through the first call or test your extensions? We’re a message away.

Why Wait?

Your team doesn’t need to live with outdated systems, bad audio quality, or high landline bills. VoIP is clearer, cheaper, and more flexible — and you can make the switch today.
