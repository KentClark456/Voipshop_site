FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Do I need fibre or LTE internet?

Yes — a stable connection (fibre or strong LTE) ensures clear, consistent call quality.

What happens if I move premises?

No problem — your system is cloud-based. We’ll help you move or reinstall at your new location.

Can I add more extensions later?

Yes — your system can grow with your business. Tell us when you’re ready and we’ll scale it up.

Can I use my existing phones?

We recommend Yealink, but if you have SIP-compatible phones, we’ll check compatibility for you.

What support is included?

Remote support is included. Need onsite help? We dispatch a technician for a flat R450 call-out.
